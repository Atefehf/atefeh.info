Vanguard

The Santa Clara Vanguard (SCV) Drum & Bugle Corps
The Santa Clara Vanguard (SCV) Drum & Bugle Corps hired us to create a website.

The challenge was to build a website to be simple and easy to navigate for all sorts of users with various missions.

After the wireframing process, I created an interface to ease accomplishing multiple tasks for multiple primary stakeholders.

I took advantage of simplicity and modern visuals to attract a young audiences.

Navigation

The decision on the hamburger menu and its challenges:
When the navigation is too long, the hamburger menu is a helpful option to incorporate a site menu without taking up too much room.
- condenses information
- achieves a minimalist aesthetic
- keeps site uncluttered

Challenge

Considering the fact that one group of users is in the age-range over 50 years old, I didnâ€™t think the hamburger menu is a smart choice for that target. I believe the main information and tasks that most users looking for, needs to be upfront and center to hook users immediately upon arrival.

Solution

Kept the hamburger menu and added 3 circles on hero image in homepage to guide users to a shortcut accomplishing their tasks.
